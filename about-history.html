Our History

We are a full service agency representing the manufacturer serving the automotive aftermarket. Through the years, we have represented manufacturers whose product lines have taken us into all market segments of our industry. The agency was established in 1971 by Robert Peterson with the goal in mind of selling the highest quality products to our entire territory customer base. We leave no stone unturned!


The success of any agency today depends on service, communications, knowledge, imagination, credibility, resourcefulness, preparedness and follow-up.


We work closely with our customers in the field at all levels of distribution and rely heavily on regular communication with our manufacturers. Our agency has achieved a reputation that allows us to call on anyone in our territory with an 'open door' reception.


Our industry has changed, our customer base has changed, and our customer's needs have changed. We, too, have changed with the times and constantly adapt to make our manufacturers successful in our territory.
