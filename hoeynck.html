Bill Hoeynck : Principal & Territory Manager


Qualifications

Covers Eastern Missouri, Southern Illinois and maintains office. Bill has been with Peterson Sales for over 25 years. During that time, he has steered the agency to over 15 straight years of sales increases. In an age of agency consolidation and companies going out of business, this is relatively unheard of. He has also managed a territory and has had over 18 years of sales increases. Bill's reputation is very good with factories and customers alike. His honesty and integrity have been winning ingredients in successfully growing the companies Peterson Sales represents.
